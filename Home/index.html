FAQ

How can HelpCreate help investors?

Find it hard to connect with budding startups? Unsure who may have the potential to grow a business better? Want to reduce the risk of investing in startups and maximize gains?
We are here to help you with just that. Seamlessly connect with startups around the world, have an in-depth insight into startups looking for funding and get the top ideas which have been validated by a community educated in ideation/innovation all at your finger tips!

How does HelpCreate benefit its members?

Ever find yourself with a revolutionary idea but don’t know who to share it with? Unsure who you can collaborate with? Don’t know how to reach investors for funding?
We understand the effort it takes for you to solve these challenges and hence exist to make all this simpler for you. We are a facebook, twitter, linkdin and whatsapp for all things startups and innovation!
